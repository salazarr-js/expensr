

# 📊 Expensr

> A modern and simple expense tracker.

## Principles

- **Simplicity First**
  > Keep it obvious, clean, and easy to use.
- **Frictionless Input**
  > Adding an expense should take seconds — no unnecessary steps, no friction.
- **Clarity Over Features**
  > Focus on meaningful insights, not feature count.
- **Built for Understanding**
  > Go beyond logging - reveal patterns and support better decisions.
- **Fast by Default**
  > Instant, responsive, and lightweight by design.
- **Your Data, Your Control**
  > No dark patterns, no tricks — your data stays yours.
